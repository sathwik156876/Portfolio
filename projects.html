AWS POWERED HEALTH HUB



The project aims to provide a comprehensive platform for blood donation, organ donation, and medical appliances donation built using the powerful capabilities of Amazon web services(AWS) for reliable storage and security. It serves as a bridge between donors and recipients, connecting individuals in need of critical resources with generous contributors who are willing to make a life-saving difference. In the Donors section, users can register themselves as potential donors, providing essential information such as their blood type, organ availability, or medical appliances they can donate. On the other hand, the Recipients section is designed for individuals who require blood, organs, or medical appliances
